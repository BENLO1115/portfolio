ABOUT US
十年職人
用料理說故事
Ten Years of Craft · Telling Stories Through Food
沐光食堂創立於 2015 年，堅持每日採購在地新鮮食材，以最簡單的方式烹調出最真實的台灣味。我們相信，一頓好飯，能讓疲憊的一天變得值得。

主廚陳師傅擁有二十年烹飪資歷，從小在外婆的廚房長大，每一道菜都藏著滿滿的家鄉記憶。
Founded in 2015, Muguang Restaurant insists on sourcing fresh local ingredients daily, preparing Taiwan's most authentic flavors in the simplest way. We believe a good meal can make even the most tiring day worthwhile.

Chef Chen brings twenty years of culinary experience, having grown up in his grandmother's kitchen. Every dish carries a cherished memory of home.
